The Process

Simple Steps.
Powerful Results.

We handle the complexity. You handle the business. Three steps from conversation to a fully running system.

01
Week 1

Consult

We kick off with a free 30-minute strategy call to understand your business, goals, and current bottlenecks. Then we map out the exact systems you need — nothing more, nothing less.

  • Business audit and gap analysis
  • Goal-setting and KPIs
  • Custom roadmap and proposal
  • Pricing and timeline confirmation
02
Weeks 2–4

Build

Our team gets to work building your systems. You'll receive regular updates and preview links throughout the build phase. We don't disappear — we keep you informed every step of the way.

  • UI/UX design mockups for approval
  • Development and AI training
  • Integrations with your existing tools
  • Internal testing and QA
03
Week 4+

Launch

We deploy your systems and stay hands-on through launch. You'll get a full walkthrough, documentation, and ongoing support. This isn't a handoff — it's a partnership.

  • Live deployment and monitoring
  • Team onboarding and training
  • Performance tracking setup
  • Ongoing optimization and support
